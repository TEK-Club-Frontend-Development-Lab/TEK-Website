Frontend Development Lab

The Frontend Development Lab offers hands-on experience in building web-based solutions with a focus on Django. Members will collaborate on real-world projects, dynamic interfaces, and responsive designs using HTML, CSS, Python, etc. The lab covers frontend design, backend logic, and database integration, fostering technical skills and teamwork.

Our Projects

Spring 2025

UUKAA Project
UUKAA (University of Utah Korean Alumni Association) is a community that connects Korean alumni of the University of Utah. The website shares news, events, and resources to strengthen ties between alumni and support the growing U of U network in Korea. Frontend Development Lab members designed and developed the frontend using HTML and CSS.
TEK Website Project
The TEK website is a full-featured platform developed from scratch by members of the Frontend Development Lab. It showcases our lab's projects, research, and activities, with a fully custom design and structure built entirely in-house. The site reflects our commitment to clean design, usability, and hands-on development experience.
